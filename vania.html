VANIA ANGELA


She was born in the year of 2002 in Jakarta.
Used to work as a profesional fashion designer for several years before she started
her career in the Rookie Gallery, She transformed her art style and gained popularity
from the the clients. But soon, she realized that she could make a better gallery,
by doing a collaboration. Together with Djonathan and Vanessa in Meraki Gallery,
she believes that art is more than what we could think for.
Now, she specify both digital and traditional painting and she is known for her "Doitsu-Kohaku" painting
as she uploaded the art work on the International KOI Painting Competition in the year of 2014
(fictional)
