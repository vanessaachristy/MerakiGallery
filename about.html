ABOUT THE GALLERY
        Our Story
                   THE BEGINNING

                   MERAKI GALLERY was established in the beginning of 2019. This gallery is previously preceded by the small art gallery called Rookie Gallery whose owner
                   is one of the artist in this Meraki Gallery, Vania. The rebranding of the Rookie Gallery provides the newer version of art cultures and exhibition. Vania decided
                   to recruit and work together with two of her friends in the art community, Vanessa and Djonathan. Now, this three musketeers have combined their passions
                   together and decided to share, communicate, and visualize their abstract and concrete ideas into artworks that are exhibited in this Meraki Gallery.
