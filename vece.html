VANESSA CHRISTY CHANDRA


She was born in Jakarta, 27th February 2002.
Raised in a family who always prioritized art and the aesthetic matters of everything,
formed her into a child who had an interest in art.
Moreover, on her junior year, she joined several digital art organization
in her city to develop her skills even more.
On her senior year, she took a part in Jakarta Digital Art Exhibiton 2017,
with her digital artwork titled
"Dualism".
From the art organization she took part in, she got a chance to meet two amazing artists,
Djonathan and Vania.
They become friends for several years, and decided to create
small organization and art exhibition consisting three of them called
MERAKI GALLERY
(fictional)
